About Timetable AI

Timetable AI is an education-focused web application designed to help college students generate clash-free academic timetables quickly and efficiently.

In many colleges, students face difficulties during course registration due to overlapping class slots, limited faculty availability, and complex schedules. Manually comparing different timetable combinations is time-consuming and error-prone.

The Problem Students Face

During course enrollment, students often have to choose between multiple sections of the same subject. Each section may be taught by a different faculty member and scheduled at different times across the week.

Students usually want:

  • No class timing clashes
  • Preferred faculty members
  • Balanced daily schedules
  • Free days when possible

Without proper tools, students rely on trial-and-error or manual comparisons, which can lead to poor scheduling decisions.

How Timetable AI Works

Timetable AI uses a structured optimization approach to analyze all possible combinations of selected courses and sections.

Users can:

  • Paste their official timetable data
  • Select the courses they want to enroll in
  • Set preferences such as avoiding early morning classes or preferring certain faculty
  • Generate multiple optimized timetable options

The system evaluates each possible timetable based on multiple factors and ranks them, helping students choose the most suitable option.

Who Is This Platform For?

Timetable AI is built primarily for:

  • Undergraduate and postgraduate college students
  • Students enrolling in institutions with complex timetable structures
  • Anyone who wants to reduce stress during course registration

Faculty Reviews and Insights

The platform also allows students to share anonymous faculty feedback. These insights help future students make informed decisions while selecting course sections.

All reviews are stored anonymously, and no personally identifiable information is displayed publicly.

Monetization Model

Timetable AI follows a transparent credit-based model.

Users can access basic functionality for free, while advanced features such as multiple timetable generation options may require purchasing credits.

Payments are handled securely through trusted third-party payment providers.

Our Goal

Our goal is to simplify academic planning and help students make confident decisions about their course schedules.

Timetable AI is continuously improving based on student feedback and real-world usage.
